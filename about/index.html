Guilherme Quintino is a ITSM Manager & IT infrastructure engineer at Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of Tocantins - Brazil. He has a BSc. in Computer Science from Federal University of Goiás (UFG) and worked with research in computational physics and bioinformatics at the Collaborative Center for Biosystems and Computational Physics at UFG. He has experience in data center architecture, linux clusters, high performance computing, VMware solutions, pfSense firewall and network engineering.

He is currently researching machine learning, neural networks, deep learning and related topics.
